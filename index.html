Purpose

WITH OUR THOUGHTS WE MAKE THE WORLD! 94GCTians trust is a nonprofit organization (independent entity) registered under Indian trust act - an initiative solely managed, funded and operated by 1990-1994 batch of Government College of Technology, Coimbatore. Beneficiaries are current students of Government College of Technology, Coimbatore, only. Objectives of the trust would be to focus on student’s sponsorship and welfare activities which will have direct positive impact on their life - an attempt to do small good things in a great way!!
